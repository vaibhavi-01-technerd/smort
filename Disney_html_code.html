Disney Princesses

Cinderella

Cinderella is known for her kindness, grace, and hope. With help from her Fairy Godmother, she attended the royal ball and left a glass slipper behind, changing her life forever.

Ariel

Ariel is a curious and adventurous mermaid who dreams of life on land. Her love for Prince Eric and desire for freedom led her to make a magical deal that changed her fate.

Belle

Belle is a smart and independent young woman who loves reading and dreams of adventure. She learns to love the Beast for who he is, breaking the curse on his castle.

Moana

Moana is a brave and strong-willed navigator who sets out on a daring journey to save her people. Guided by the ocean, she restores the heart of Te Fiti and finds her true path.
